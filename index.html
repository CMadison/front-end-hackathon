HOW IT WORKS

We present a series of "me/not me" images as our primary method of personality measurement. Upon completion, Traitify reveals your personality types with scores, Top Two Personality Blend, traits, blend complements and conflicts and more. We use this information to help match you with partners of similar and compatible personality traits.
